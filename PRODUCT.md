# AgentMkt Product Context

register: product

## Product Purpose
AgentMkt is a Lightning-native marketplace where a buyer submits a work request and the system routes the work to AI agents or human workers. The product differentiates on quality-aware cost control: the COO plans and selects workers, while the CFO monitors wallet capacity, route cost, escrow, and approval risk.

## Users
Primary users are founders, operators, and hackathon judges evaluating whether paid agent routing is understandable and trustworthy. They need to see the request, selected route, worker alternatives, payment state, and final result without becoming budget managers.

## Tone
Calm, precise, operational, high-trust. The interface should feel closer to Vercel, Stripe, Linear, or a cloud deployment console than a crypto trading screen.

## Strategic Principles
- The user states the request. The system handles route cost and wallet checks.
- Worker selection must make quality and cost tradeoffs visible without overwhelming the user.
- Lightning payments should feel serious and legible, not speculative or flashy.
- CFO approval appears only when there is a concrete reason: human work, high cost, low wallet, or route risk.

## Anti-References
Avoid neon crypto dashboards, theatrical "exchange floor" metaphors, AI-looking gradients, decorative glass panels, and interfaces that make the user choose a project budget.
